A Sweet Story of First Love

"Flipped" (2010) is one of those quiet little movies that really sticks with you. It’s about two neighbors, Juli and Bryce, and how their feelings for each other shift as they grow up. Directed by Rob Reiner, the film captures all the awkwardness, excitement, and heartbreak of first love.

Why I Love It

What makes this movie special is that it tells the story from both Juli's and Bryce’s points of view. You get to see how each of them interprets the same moments in totally different ways, which is both fun and emotional. It's soft, nostalgic, and surprisingly deep for such a simple story.

Fun Things to Know

  • The movie is based on the novel "Flipped" by Wendelin Van Draanen.
  • Rob Reiner, the director, also made other romcom classics like "When Harry Met Sally."
  • The neighborhood in the movie became a real fan-favorite spot to visit after the film came out.

Favorite Quote
