A High School Romcom That Still Hits

"10 Things I Hate About You" came out in 1999, and even though it's been years, it still feels fresh. It’s a teen romcom loosely based on Shakespeare’s "The Taming of the Shrew", but don’t worry!! It’s way more fun than it sounds. With Heath Ledger and Julia Stiles in the lead, it gives you iconic moments, killer chemistry, and some surprisingly emotional scenes.

Why I Love It

This movie has the perfect mix of sarcasm, sweet romance, and real emotions. The characters aren’t just one-dimensional, they actually grow throughout the story. And come on, that scene where Patrick sings "Can’t Take My Eyes Off You" on the bleachers? Instant classic.

Fun Things You Might Not Know

  • Heath Ledger actually did some of his own skateboarding stunts in the film.
  • The emotional poem Kat reads at the end? That came straight from the heart, Julia Stiles said she cried for real while filming it.
  • The high school in the movie is a real one in Tacoma, Washington; it used to be a hotel!

Most Iconic Moment

"I hate the way you talk to me, and the way you cut your hair. I hate the way you drive my car, I hate it when you stare."
— Kat Stratford
